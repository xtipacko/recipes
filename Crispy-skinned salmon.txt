Ingredients:
 4 (200g each) salmon fillets, skin on
 2 tablespoons olive oil 
 1 teaspoon salt flakes
 Steamed chat potatoes, to serve 
 Salad leaves, to serve
Cooking METHOD:
Step 1: Place salmon, skin side up, on a plate. Drizzle with oil. Rub salt into skin.
Step 2: Heat a large, non-stick frying pan over medium-high heat. Add salmon, skin side down. Cook for 4 to 5 minutes or until skin is crisp. Turn. Cook, covered for 4 to 5 minutes for medium or until cooked to your liking.
Step 3: Serve with potatoes and salad leaves.
